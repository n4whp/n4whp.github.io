About Me

My journey in amateur radio began on January 18, 2024, with my first contact, KQ4GGH. My Uncle, Gene Payne (W4YSP), got me into the hobby and gave me my first two radios: an ICOM-746PRO and a Baofeng BF-F8HP.

I'm now a certified SKYWARN Spotter and love exploring new frequencies and making contacts.
