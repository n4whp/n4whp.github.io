Buckingham County 2m Net

@ 147.550 MHz FM & 145.290 -6 131.8 PL

About

This net was created to serve the Buckingham County area and portions of the surrounding areas.

Info

The net meets on Saturdays at 8 PM on 147.550 MHz FM.

The net also meets on Sundays at 8 PM on 145.290 -6 131.8 PL MHz FM

On week days @ 8PM on 147.550 there will be a round table of info sharing with no Net Control..

Please read our Basic Net Protocols & Rules

This net is not endorsed by county officials it is run by the citizens!!!!
